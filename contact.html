Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions About Our Courses

How do I access course materials after purchase?

After purchasing a course, you'll receive login credentials via email. Simply sign in to your account on our platform, and you'll have immediate access to all course materials, including videos, workbooks, and bonus resources.

Are there any prerequisites for your courses?

Most of our courses are designed for entrepreneurs at all levels. For beginners, we provide foundational knowledge, while more experienced business owners can skip ahead to advanced strategies. Some specialized courses may have specific prerequisites, which will be clearly mentioned in the course description.

Do you offer refunds if I'm not satisfied?

Yes, we stand behind our courses with a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, simply contact our support team within 30 days, and we'll process a full refund, no questions asked.

How long do I have access to the course?

Once you purchase a course, you'll have lifetime access to all course materials. This includes any future updates or additional content that may be added to the course over time.

Is there any live support or coaching included?

Our standard courses include access to our private community forum where you can ask questions and get support. Premium courses and coaching programs include direct access to instructors through group coaching calls or one-on-one sessions, depending on the specific program.
